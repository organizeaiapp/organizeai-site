🗂️ OrganizeAI

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective Date: March 21, 2026

Thank you for using OrganizeAI. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. We built OrganizeAI with privacy in mind — most of your data never leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

OrganizeAI is a Chrome browser extension that helps you organize your AI chat conversations across platforms like Gemini and ChatGPT. It is operated by OrganizeAI (organizeaiapp@gmail.com).

2. Data We Collect

a) Local storage (Free users) Stays on your device

When you use the free version of OrganizeAI, all data is stored exclusively in your browser using chrome.storage.local. This includes:

  • Folder names you create
  • Chat IDs and titles you assign to folders
  • Your extension preferences and settings

This data is never transmitted to our servers and never leaves your device.

b) Cloud storage (Pro users, when available) Opt-in only

If you opt in to a Pro subscription, the following data may be stored in our cloud to enable sync and backup features:

  • Folder names
  • Chat IDs associated with your folders
  • Your account email address

We will always ask for your explicit consent before enabling cloud sync. You can delete your cloud data at any time by contacting us.

c) Email address (Waitlist / Early Access) Optional

If you voluntarily submit your email to join our Pro waitlist or early access program, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date you signed up

This is entirely optional. You will only receive product updates and launch announcements. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the OrganizeAI extension
  • To sync your folders across devices (Pro users only, with consent)
  • To send product updates and launch announcements (only if you opted in)
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • We never use your data for advertising

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which may process your email address if you opt in to our waitlist:

  • Google Forms / Google Sheets – used to collect and store waitlist signups. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Brevo (brevo.com) – used to send product update emails to waitlist subscribers. Unsubscribe links are included in every email and managed automatically. Subject to Brevo's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data – retained in your browser until you uninstall the extension or clear browser storage.
  • Cloud data (Pro) – retained until you request deletion.
  • Email (waitlist) – retained until you unsubscribe or request removal.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in any email
  • Opt out of cloud sync (Pro users) at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at organizeaiapp@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

OrganizeAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
